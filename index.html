Our Story

The Full Circle Difference

We started Full Circle Retrievers right here in New Jersey with a simple belief: the right dog changes everything. A retriever that fits your family, your lifestyle, and your home isn't an accident — it's the result of careful breeding, early socialization, and a breeder who stays in your corner long after pickup day.

Our name says it all. We see the relationship between breeder, dog, and family as a full circle — one we take seriously from the first whelp to the last gray muzzle. We health-test every parent, temperament-evaluate every pup, and stay available for the life of every dog we place.

Licensed in New Jersey and proud members of the AKC Bred with H.E.A.R.T. program, we do this the right way, every time.

What We Offer

More Than Just Puppies

We support our families before, during, and long after they bring their dog home.

🐶

Puppy Placement

Every pup is temperament-tested at 7 weeks, vet-checked, vaccinated, and microchipped before going home. We match puppies to families intentionally — not first-come, first-served.

🏠

Early Neurological Stimulation

Our puppies receive ENS and early socialization from day 3 through 16, setting the neurological foundation for confident, adaptable adult dogs.

📋

Health Guarantees

We stand behind our dogs with a written health guarantee against genetic conditions, backed by full OFA clearances on both parents before any breeding.

📞

Lifetime Breeder Support

Bought a puppy from us five years ago? We're still here. Training questions, health concerns, or just a photo update — our families always have a line back to us.
